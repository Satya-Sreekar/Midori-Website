# Midori - Cafe & Restaurant Website

## Brand Guidelines (from Midori Brand Guidlines.pdf)

### Logo
- Premium, minimalist typographic design
- The letter "I" is cleverly inverted to resemble an exclamation mark — symbolizes excitement and emphasis
- 80px safe area must be maintained around the logo on all sides
- Monochromatic (black/white) versions exist but are ONLY for specific printing requirements — do not use on the website
